Beautiful Thailand

Thailand is a mecca for rock-climbing and after working at Ping Yang Pow (PYP) I've grown an affinity for traditional Thai food, so long as it's not too spicy!

My friends that have gone there said

  • You can sit in front of a (climbing) project for a month and not get bored
  • Don't get cocky about your spice level, when they say Thai spice they mean it!
  • It's really cheap which is good on your wallet but can be hard to see, gives a lot of perspective
  • Buy a couple of nice suits when you're there, you can get 5 for $600 instead of 1 for $5,000
